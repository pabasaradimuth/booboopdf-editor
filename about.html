About BOO BOO PDF

Welcome to BOO BOO PDF, a fast, free, and secure tool designed to simplify your document management. Whether you need to merge, split, or compress files, our mission is to provide high-quality PDF tools without the need for expensive software.

Our Story

I am a first-year undergraduate student at the South Eastern University of Sri Lanka (SEUSL). This website started as a self-learning project to improve my web development skills and solve a common problem: finding easy-to-use PDF tools that respect user privacy.

"We believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone, especially students and educators, without compromising data security."

Why Choose Us?

Unlike many online editors, BOO BOO PDF processes files using client-side technology. This means your documents stay in your browser and are never stored on our servers, ensuring your private information remains private.
