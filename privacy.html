Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At BOO BOO PDF, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data and your PDF files.

1. PDF File Privacy (Local Processing)

Unlike many other online tools, BOO BOO PDF is designed for maximum security. Most of our tools use HTML and JavaScript libraries that process your files directly in your browser. This means:

  • Your files are not uploaded to a permanent database.
  • We do not read, view, or store the content of your documents.
  • Files are processed temporarily and are immediately cleared once you close the session.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require user registration. However, we may collect non-personal information such as browser type, device type, and the time of your visit to improve our website performance.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

We plan to use Google AdSense to show advertisements on this site to support our studies. Please note the following:

  • Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this site.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie allows it to serve personalized ads to users.
  • Users may opt out by visiting the Google Ad Privacy Policy.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact the creator (a student at SEUSL) via the contact details provided on our "About" page.
